RISK CLASSIFICATION

Understanding Our Risk Zones

Every area is classified into one of three risk categories based on AI analysis.

🟢

Low Risk

Score: 0 – 35

Minimal crime activity. Generally safe for residence, travel, and daily activities. Rare incidents only.

🟡

Medium Risk

Score: 36 – 65

Moderate crime activity. Petty crimes may occur. Caution advised, especially at night in isolated areas.

🔴

High Risk

Score: 66 – 100

Significant crime activity detected. Serious incidents reported. Avoid if possible — safer alternatives suggested.
